What's a Rabbit to Do??

Robert moves the rock back

Robert takes a good look at the rock. Maybe if he can push it with his nose? He tries and tries but cannot. Maybe if he could use his front legs? Robert rises up onto his backlegs and tries to push the rock, but it's no use. Just as he's about to give up and go back down on all fours, however, the rock starts to turn, just a little at first and then more. In no time the rock is back in place like nothing happened, but Robert can't stop thinking about the hole he now knows is there...

Go again!
