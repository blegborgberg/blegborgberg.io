Robert shakes his fluffy tail and turns away from the hole. He heads to the backdoor and starts to make a spectacle of himself so his people will come. He prances and butts the door, but they don't see him and he starts to feel huffy. Robert decided he'll just have to fix the hole himself. He approaches the hole and the misplaced rock with some nervousness.
